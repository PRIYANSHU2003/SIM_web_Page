Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the hospital information provided?

Our app ensures up-to-date and accurate hospital data through regular verification processes. We strive to maintain the accuracy of information to help users make informed decisions.

Is Nurse available in all regions?

Currently, Nurse is available in select regions. We aim to expand our coverage to more areas in the near future to assist a wider user base.

Can I save my favorite hospitals?

Yes, Nurse allows users to bookmark their preferred hospitals for quick access and seamless navigation. You can easily save and access your favorite hospitals within the app.

How can Nurse benefit me in emergencies?

In emergencies, Nurse provides immediate access to nearby hospitals and essential medical information, helping users make swift decisions and receive timely care.
